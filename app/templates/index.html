FAQs

How does URL shortening work?

URL shortening works by taking a long URL and creating a shorter, unique alias. When someone clicks on the shortened URL, they are redirected to the original long URL.

Is URL shortening safe?

Yes, URL shortening is safe as long as you use a reputable service. However, it's important to be cautious when clicking on shortened links from unknown sources, as they could potentially lead to malicious websites.

Can I customize my shortened URLs?

Yes, our platform allows you to customize your shortened URLs with a unique alias that reflects your brand or content.
